# crewai_email

**Automated Email Processing for Bank Loan Collection**

An intelligent AI-powered email management system designed for banks and financial institutions to automate responses to loan collection inquiries. Simply provide your email credentials and app password, and let the system automatically fetch, analyze, categorize, and respond to customer emails—eliminating human delays and improving response times.

## 🎯 Purpose

This tool is built for **bank loan collection teams** to:
- **Auto-Fetch Emails** Automatically process high volumes of customer emails
- **AI Analysis** – Analyze loan-related inquiries and customer intent of making payment.
- **Smart Categorization**- Categorize emails as willing to pay, unable to pay, asking for settlement and others.
- **Auto-Response Generation**- Generate professional, contextual responses instantly
- **Zero Human Delay** -Reduce manual workload and response delays
-  **Secure** – Credentials handled securely via environment variables
